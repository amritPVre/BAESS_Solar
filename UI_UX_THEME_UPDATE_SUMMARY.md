## ðŸ’¡ Design Philosophy

**Matte Dark Theme Principles:**
- **Depth through subtle gradients** (not flat black)
- **Glow effects instead of shadows** (using blur and opacity)
- **Translucent borders** (creates layered feel)
- **Consistent cyan accent** (ties everything together)
- **Reduced saturation** (matte, not glossy)
- **Backdrop blur** (depth and premium feel)

This creates a cohesive, modern, premium feel throughout the application while maintaining excellent readability and usability.
