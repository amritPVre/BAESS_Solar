# BAESS Labs Favicon - AI Image Generation Prompt

## Primary Prompt (Recommended)

```
Create a modern, minimalist favicon design for BAESS Labs - a solar energy intelligence platform. 

Design requirements:
- Simple geometric icon that works at 16x16px and 32x32px sizes
- Incorporate a stylized sun with clean rays or a solar panel motif
- Use a vibrant orange-to-yellow gradient representing "Sun to Shadow" philosophy
- Add subtle blue or teal accent to represent technology/intelligence
- Icon should be recognizable and distinct even at tiny sizes
- Modern, professional, tech-forward aesthetic
- Square format with slight padding
- Transparent or white background
- Bold, clean lines that remain crisp when scaled down

Color palette:
- Primary: Orange (#FF6B35) to Yellow (#FFB347) gradient
- Accent: Deep Blue (#1E3A8A) or Teal (#14B8A6)
- Optional: White highlights for depth

Style: Flat design, geometric, modern, tech startup aesthetic
Icon type: Abstract sun/energy symbol, NOT literal solar panels
```

## Alternative Prompts

### Option 2: Letter Mark
```
Design a modern monogram favicon combining letters "B" and "S" (BAESS) with a sun/energy theme. Orange-yellow gradient with geometric shapes. Minimalist, professional, tech-forward. Must be legible at 16px.
```

### Option 3: Abstract Energy Symbol
```
Create an abstract energy icon - circular form with radiating geometric rays, suggesting both solar power and digital intelligence. Vibrant orange-to-yellow gradient. Modern flat design. Perfect for 32x32px favicon.
```

## Technical Specifications

**Size Requirements:**
- Primary: 32x32px (standard favicon)
- Also create: 16x16px, 180x180px (Apple touch), 192x192px (Android)

**Format:**
- Output as SVG for scalability
- Also provide PNG with transparent background
- ICO format for browser compatibility

**Usage:**
- Main site favicon
- Browser tab icon
- Bookmark icon
- PWA icon (if applicable)

## Brand Guidelines

**BAESS Labs represents:**
- Solar intelligence
- Clean energy technology
- Data-driven solutions
- Professional consulting
- Innovation in renewable energy

**Avoid:**
- Overly complex designs
- Realistic imagery
- Text that's unreadable at small sizes
- Dark or muted colors
- Generic solar panel clipart

## Testing

After generation, test the favicon by:
1. Viewing at actual size (16x16px, 32x32px)
2. Checking contrast on both light and dark browser UI
3. Ensuring it's distinguishable from competitors
4. Verifying it remains recognizable when pinned/bookmarked

---

**Recommended AI Tools:**
- DALL-E 3
- Midjourney (use --ar 1:1 --style raw)
- Adobe Firefly
- Stable Diffusion with ControlNet

**Post-Processing:**
- Use Figma or Adobe Illustrator to convert to ICO
- Optimize with favicon-generator tools
- Test across browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
