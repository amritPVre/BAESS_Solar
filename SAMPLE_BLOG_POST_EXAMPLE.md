**The AI Solution:**
Modern AI systems can now analyze solar designs and automatically generate itemized BOQs in minutes. **The Challenge:**
Accurate energy yield predictions are critical for financial modeling and customer confidence. Traditional methods require deep knowledge of the PVWatts API and hours of manual configuration.

**The AI Advantage:**

## 5. 3D Modeling and Near-Shading Analysis

**The Technical Challenge:**
Shading analysis is crucial for accurate energy predictions, but creating 3D site models and running shading simulations has traditionally required specialized software and significant engineering time.

**The Emerging Solution:**
Next-generation AI tools are introducing automated 3D scene creation from satellite imagery and site photos. These systems can:
- Generate 3D site models from 2D inputs
- Identify potential shading obstacles (trees, buildings, chimneys)

- Optimize panel placement to minimize losses

While this technology is still emerging, early implementations are showing promising results in reducing design time while improving accuracy.
