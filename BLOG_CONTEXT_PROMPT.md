## Writing Instructions

### Tone & Style
- **Professional yet accessible**: Write for solar professionals, engineers, installers, and energy enthusiasts
- **Educational first**: Provide genuine value before promoting
- **Subtle integration**: BAESS should feel like a natural solution, not a forced advertisement
- **Data-driven**: Use statistics, industry trends, and technical insights
- **Future-forward**: Emphasize innovation, AI, and the future of renewable energy

### Content Guidelines

1. **Introduction (Hook)**
   - Start with a compelling industry problem, trend, or question
   - Mention current challenges in solar design/energy storage (e.g., time-consuming calculations, complex BOQs, manual processes)

2. **Body Content**
   - Provide educational content related to the topic
   - When discussing solutions, challenges, or workflows, naturally introduce BAESS features as examples or solutions
   - Use phrases like:
     - "Modern platforms like [BAESS Labs](https://www.baess.app)..."
     - "AI-powered tools such as [BAESS Labs' PV Designer Pro](https://www.baess.app/products/pv-designer)..."
     - "Advanced solutions like [BAESS Designer](https://www.baess.app/products/bess-designer)..."
     - "Innovative platforms are now offering..."
   - Include 2-3 contextual hyperlinks to:
     - Homepage: https://www.baess.app
     - PV Designer: https://www.baess.app/products/pv-designer
     - BESS Designer: https://www.baess.app/products/bess-designer

3. **Problem → Solution Pattern**
   - Identify industry pain points (manual calculations, time waste, errors, complexity)
   - Present BAESS features as part of the solution
   - Example: "Solar installers traditionally spend 4-6 hours creating BOQs manually. AI-powered platforms like [BAESS Labs](https://www.baess.app) now generate comprehensive BOQs in minutes using Google Gemini 2.0."

4. **Use Cases & Examples**
   - When relevant, mention specific use cases:
     - Residential solar installations (3kW-10kW)
     - Commercial rooftop systems (50kW-500kW)
     - Utility-scale projects (1MW+)
     - Energy storage integration
   - Show how BAESS solves real problems in these scenarios

5. **Technical Credibility**
   - Reference BAESS's technical foundation:
     - "NREL-verified calculations"
     - "Best financial standards followed globally"
     - "Google Gemini 2.0 AI integration"
     - "Design capacity from 10kWh to 100MWh+"

6. **Call-to-Action (Subtle)**
   - End with a soft CTA that provides value
   - Examples:
     - "Explore how AI is transforming solar design at [BAESS Labs](https://www.baess.app)"
     - "See how [AI-powered BOQ generation](https://www.baess.app/products/pv-designer) can streamline your workflow"
     - "Learn more about [intelligent energy storage design](https://www.baess.app/products/bess-designer)"
